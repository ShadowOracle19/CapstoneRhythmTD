The Gameplay of our v0.3 demo is plants vs zombies meets crypt of the necrodancer

To launch the game play the Dissonance.exe file 

The game has basic dialogue upon playing level 1-1 as well in the level select screen
there are character specific sample dialogue showing off our dialogue system as a whole.

Our tower defense system has enemies and tower projectiles moving to the beat as well as 
player movement with a cursor. As towers are placed more tracks to the song starts to play.

Game controls:
Arrow Keys to move on beat
SPACE to open tower placement menu
	Arrow keys to select tower
ESCAPE to pause game

Warning audio may be loud on first playthrough
